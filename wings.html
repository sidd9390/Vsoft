Elevate Your Banking Operations with WINGS

Welcome to WINGS, an advanced core banking solution designed to transform the way banks operate. With WINGS, banks can automate their processes, enhance customer experiences, and achieve operational excellence. Experience the power of WINGS and soar to new heights in the banking industry.

WINGS: Revolutionizing Core Banking

WINGS is a comprehensive core banking solution that enables banks to manage their operations seamlessly. With its robust features, advanced technology, and user-friendly interface, WINGS empowers banks to stay competitive, enhance efficiency, and deliver superior services to their customers.

Key Features and Benefits

Integrated Banking Operations

WINGS brings together all critical banking functions, including account management, transaction processing, loan management, treasury operations, and more, in a unified platform.

Customer-Centric Approach

WINGS prioritizes customer satisfaction by providing a 360-degree view of customer, enabling personalized services, and empowering banks to build lasting relationships.

Key Features and Benefits

Multi-Channel Support

WINGS supports a wide range of channels, including mobile banking, internet banking, branch operations, and self-service kiosks, ensuring seamless access and consistent experiences across all touchpoints.

Real-Time Processing

WINGS enables real-time transaction processing, ensuring prompt and accurate updates to customer accounts, balances, and financial data, enhancing operational efficiency.

Key Features and Benefits

Robust Security and Compliance

WINGS incorporates advanced security measures and complies with industry regulations, safeguarding customer data, and ensuring a secure banking environment.

Scalable and Flexible

WINGS is designed to adapt to the evolving needs of banks, whether they are small or mid-scale operations or large banking enterprises. The solution grows with your business.

WINGS Modules

Account Management

Effortlessly manage customer accounts, track balances, transactions, and account activities, ensuring accurate and up-to-date financial information.

Transaction Processing

Enable a wide range of transactions, covering all types of deposits, withdrawals, fund transfers, bill payments, and more, with seamless integration into other banking systems.

WINGS Modules

Loan Origination System

the LOS is a robust and scalable solution that consists of configurable workflow and rules engine, application processing and management, decision-making based on predefined criteria and advanced credit scoring models, Document Management and Collateral Management. By leveraging advanced technology, intelligent workflows, and data analytics, our system enhances efficiency, reduces processing time, and minimizes risk.

Loan Management

Streamline the loan lifecycle, from application and approval to disbursement, servicing, and collections. Automate loan processes, manage collateral, and mitigate risk effectively.

WINGS Modules

Treasury Operations

Empower banks to manage treasury operations, including trading, investments, risk management, and liquidity management, optimizing returns while ensuring compliance.

Data vault

Data Vault is a cutting-edge hardware security encryption solution that ensures the utmost protection for your critical data. By combining advanced encryption algorithms, tamper-resistant hardware, and robust key management, Data Vault delivers data security to safeguard against unauthorized access and breaches
